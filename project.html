Exploring Jane Eyre: An Interactive Visualization of Themes and Topics

By Carla Viteri

Introduction

Welcome to an interactive exploration of themes and topics in Charlotte BrontÃ«'s Jane Eyre. This project aims to leverage topic modeling techniques to uncover thematic structures within the novel. By analyzing how different chapters contribute to identified topics, we can gain a deeper understanding of the novel's underlying themes and their evolution throughout the story.

This explorable explanation will guide you through the topic modeling process, provide interactive visualizations, and offer insights into how well the topics generated by the LDA model align with the content of the chapters in Jane Eyre.

Analysis of Topics and Their Themes

Based on the topic modeling results and their associated probabilities across chapters, we can infer possible themes for each topic and correlate them with the content of the chapters in which they appear.

Intertopic Distance Map and Top-30 Most Relevant Terms for Topic

The Intertopic Distance Map provides a visualization of the relationships between the identified topics. Each circle represents a topic, with the size of the circle indicating the overall prevalence of the topic in the novel. The proximity of the circles reflects the similarity between the topics.

Sentiment Distribution Across Topics

The Sentiment Bubble Chart visualizes the sentiment scores of the topics. Each bubble represents a topic, with the size indicating the number of chapters contributing to the topic. The color of the bubble reflects the average sentiment score, helping us understand the emotional tone associated with each topic.

Topic Evolution Over Chapters

Using the Latent Dirichlet Allocation (LDA) model, we have identified several topics within "Jane Eyre." Each topic consists of a cluster of words that frequently appear together, indicating specific themes in the text. We analyzed how these topics are distributed across different chapters. The Topic Evolution Line Chart shows how the prevalence of each topic changes over the chapters of the novel. This visualization helps us track the development of themes throughout the narrative, providing insights into how the story's focus shifts over time.

Highlighted Text Passages

The highlighted text passages provide an interactive way to explore how specific words contribute to the identified topics. Conclusion

This project demonstrates the power of topic modeling in uncovering the thematic structure of a literary work. By applying LDA to Jane Eyre, we have identified key themes and tracked their evolution throughout the novel. The interactive visualizations provide a dynamic way to explore these themes and gain deeper insights into Charlotte BrontÃ«'s masterpiece.

The results show a strong alignment between the generated topics and the novel's content, validating the effectiveness of our approach. This interactive exploration not only enhances our understanding of the novel but also showcases the potential of data visualization and natural language processing in literary analysis.
