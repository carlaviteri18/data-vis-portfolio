By Carla Viteri

Introduction

In this project, we use topic modeling to explore the thematic structure of Charlotte BrontÃ«'s novel "Jane Eyre." Through interactive visualizations, we examine how different themes are distributed throughout the text, providing deeper insights into the novel's content.

Analysis of Topics and Their Themes

Using the Latent Dirichlet Allocation (LDA) model, we have identified several topics within "Jane Eyre." Each topic consists of a cluster of words that frequently appear together, indicating specific themes in the text. We analyzed how these topics are distributed across different chapters.
